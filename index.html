Start with an account, then pay only for active work.

The new Kall flow is deposit-based. Users sign up before requesting, charge a balance, set a maximum minute limit, and receive an itemized time summary after each request.

1

Create account

Collect only full name, email, and phone number. No password or membership application is required on this page.

2

Add deposit

Choose a package and pay by credit card, PayPal, or Korean bank transfer. The balance converts into usable Kall minutes.

3

Send request

Submit the venue, date, people, notes, and a maximum time limit. Kall confirms before accepting third-party fees.

4

Track usage

Every completed request shows minutes used, remaining balance, call result, and refund-eligible unused credit.

Deposit packages with clear per-minute rates.

These packages replace the old one-time booking fee. The larger the deposit, the lower the per-minute rate and the longer the account validity window.

Transparent charging and refund rules.

Kall should state exactly what is charged, what is not charged, and how unused deposit credit is refunded.

Time charged

Only active handling time is deducted.

    Time not charged

    No deduction when Kall is not actively working.

      Refund policy: Unused eligible deposit credit can be refunded to the original payment method or by bank transfer after identity and payment verification. Used minutes, third-party costs already paid with your approval, and non-refundable processor or FX fees are not refunded unless the provider returns them to Kall. Starter and Standard balances are automatically scheduled for refund at the end of their validity window unless you top up to extend the account. Promotional bonus minutes expire and have no cash value.
